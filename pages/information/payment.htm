The first step of becoming a part of BAUMUN 2016 is to complete the payment procedure. Before making their payments, participants are required to read through the payment details and requirements provided below and additional information that the acceptance e-mail will entail. Please follow the abovementioned steps for correct completion:

  1. Please complete your payment within 5 days after receiving your Acceptance Letter by the Public Relations Team. The account information will be provided in the e-mail.
    While making your payment, please make sure that you specify BAUMUN and your NAME as part of the explanation of the money transfer ( BAUMUN – Emre Polatcan ).
  2. After completion of the payment, please send a copy of your receipt to finance@baumun.org.
    Your application will only be valid after we have received your payment received, otherwise your application will be cancelled.
  3. The applicant(s) will receive their allocated country/position & committee.

Important Reminders:

  • Delegations should pay their application fees in just one payment as a whole, including all the delegates and observers! Individuals of delegations shouldn’t make their payments by themselves.
  • Any transaction fees for the bank transfer will be the applicant’s responsibility, and incomplete payments cannot be accepted as a valid payment.
  • Please note that BAUMUN holds a no-refund policy.
  • The Finance Team holds the right to change the conference fees to be able to set the exchange rate to a pre-determined rate.
  • The conference fee covers:
    • Snacks & drinks during coffee breaks
    • Transportation to and from BAUMUN social events during set times
    • Certificate of Participation
    • Badges & placards
    • BAUMUN stationery
    • Lunches during the conference days
    • Gala Dinner at the Bosphorus
